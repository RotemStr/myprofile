The sky is the limit...

My name is Rotem, and I immigrated to Israel from Minsk, the capital of Belarus, in 1999. I spent my first few years in Israel living in Kibbutz Tzora and studying at The Hebrew University Secondary School, also known as LEYADA, in Jerusalem. After completing school, I served in the military where I discovered my passion for computers and technology. As a System Administrator, my responsibilities included workstation and network installation, configuration, user support, as well as backend management, which included server backup and ongoing maintenance.
Unfortunately, due to some unforeseen circumstances, I was unable to pursue my studies after completing my military service. As a result, I ventured into the fashion and sales industries, which had always intrigued me. I spent many years working in these sectors, but after a while, I realized that this was not my ultimate aspiration in life. I knew that I had more potential within me, and I was determined to do better. So, I made the decision to pick up where I left off and start anew from that point. Finally, I have reached where I wanted to be - a new beginning!
